THE LAST DAY
You start off in the higher levels of the space station, where all the important people go. There doesn’t seem to be many people around for some reason. In fact, you haven’t seen a single person at all on the way up here. You could go all the way to the loading docks and steal a spacecraft to explore space. You can’t get enough of it and you suppose it’s a good thing you’d never finish exploring all of it in your lifetime.
